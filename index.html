I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Kannur University, a position I have held since June 2023.

Prior to this role, I pursued postdoctoral fellowships at prestigious institutions. At the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore, I served as a Research Associate and Visiting Scientist (December 2022–June 2023) and earlier as a Visiting Scientist (September–November 2022). My postdoctoral experience also includes positions at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, where I worked as a National Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Associate (March 2020–August 2022) and as a Research Associate (August–October 2019). Additionally, I contributed as a Visiting Scientist at the Kerala School of Mathematics, Kozhikode, in June–July 2019.

I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER), Bhubaneswar, in 2020. Earlier, I earned an M.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Hyderabad (2012) and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Calicut, Kerala (2010).

Programs organized

Summer Workshop in Mathematics (SWIM@KSoM)

I am one of the organizers of the Summer Workshop in Mathematics (SWIM@KSoM), a rigorous instructional program held at Kannur University (Thavakkara campus). The workshop provides an in-depth exploration of fundamental areas like Mathematical Analysis, Abstract Algebra, and Linear Algebra, complemented by problem-solving sessions. The next edition is scheduled from May 5, 2025 to May 30, 2025.

For more information, visit the official website: swim-ksom.github.io
