I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Kannur University, since June 2023.

My postdoctoral journey includes roles at Indian Statistical Institute and IISc Bangalore. I received my Ph.D. from NISER Bhubaneswar in 2020.

I hold an M.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Hyderabad (2012) and a B.Sc. from the University of Calicut (2010).
